Reviews about Alien Isolation

Alien isolation is a game about the Xenomorph franchise, it involves AI movement to find and challenge the player.

The players objective is to evade the Xenomorph and find answers about your characters mother or seperate mission depending on the gamemode.

I chose this game as it has been very enjoyable and challenging to me and it involves an aspect of horror which I love in videogames.

Overall I would suggest trying this game out as their AI movement is quite unique and certainly challenging to begin with. Additionally, you could also play as a Xenomorph and try out that playstyle as well.
